
a) top edit field is the stream name the av stream will be published to
b) second edit field is wowza address

1)extract the contents of the zip file to a directory on your hard drive
2)launch the simpleaudiotest.exe application (dbl click on exe)
3)click the connect button - this will start up your video camera and audio capture device as well as negotiate the connection with wowza. If your hardware started up correctly you will see a video image (upside down is correct) and the audio graph will show audio. If you are watching your wowza logs you should also see the stream publish events.
4)clicking the send video / send audio will toggle the actual sending of video and audio to the wowza server.
